Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Chocolate, Peanut Butter and Bananas are great choices for making Smoothies and a delightful way to pack in nutrition and flavor

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 cup almond milk or milk of choice

Method:

  1. Blend until you achieve a smooth consistency.
  2. You can adjust the thickness by adding more or less liquid.
